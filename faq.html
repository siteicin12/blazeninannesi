Frequently Asked Questions

What is Damned's estimated release date?

Damned is still in mid-development and away from release. Please, check our announcements (in-game) for more information. We will announce the release date as soon as we can.

What game engine are you using to develop Damned?

Damned was made using Unity, a powerful and professional game engine that is widely compatible with most platforms.

What software do you use for pixel art animations?

We use Pyxel Edit for our pixel art animations. It is an excellent software with all the simple functions needed to draw pixel art, set up sprite sheets, and preview animations. It also allows for layer management and tile placement.

What software do you use for environment art?

We use Photoshop for mockups and Pyxel Edit to set up tilesets for our environment art.

How can I support Damned's development?

You can support Damned's development by donating to our Ko-fi pages and sharing the game with your friends. You may also help us by finding bugs, reporting them to us, or suggesting new features.
